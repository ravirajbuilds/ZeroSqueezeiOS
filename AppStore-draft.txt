5. DESCRIPTION (4000)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Your heartbeat, in numbers — using nothing but your phone.

ZeroSqueeze turns your iPhone into a calm, private vitals check. Look into
the motion sensor, or rest a fingertip on the rear camera and flash, and
ZeroSqueeze reads the tiny color changes blood makes as it moves through
you. In seconds you get your heart rate, heart-rate variability,
breathing rate, and an estimate of your hemoglobin — every number
explained in plain language, all of it computed on your device.

No wearable. No chest strap. No cuff. No needle. No account. Nothing is
ever uploaded.

— A 30-SECOND CHECK-IN —
• Chest scan: hold the phone at eye level for ~30 seconds. ZeroSqueeze
  estimates heart rate, beat-to-beat variability (HRV) and respiration
  from the subtle shifts in your skin.
• Fingertip scan: cover the rear camera and flash for ~20 seconds for an
  estimate of hemoglobin with a confidence range, plus your pulse.

— NUMBERS THAT MEAN SOMETHING —
Every reading comes with context: what's typical, where you sit, and what
it suggests — the way a good coach would tell you, not a wall of stats.
A live preview and quality meter show you when the signal is strong.

— SEE YOUR TREND —
One reading is noise; a trend is signal. ZeroSqueeze keeps a private history
of your heart rate, resting heart rate, HRV and hemoglobin so you can
watch the direction, not chase a single number.

— MAKE IT YOURS —
Have a recent lab CBC? Enter your real hemoglobin once and ZeroSqueeze
anchors future estimates to you, tightening the range.

— PRIVATE BY DESIGN —
ZeroSqueeze has no servers to talk to — there is no networking code in the
app. Every pixel is processed locally, no photo or video is ever saved,
there is no sign-in, and there is no tracking. Your readings live only on
your phone, protected when it's locked.

— BUILT WITH CARE —
A warm, modern design with full Dynamic Type and VoiceOver support, light
and dark themes, and a single daily snapshot of your vitals at a glance.

— PLEASE READ —
ZeroSqueeze gives a wellness estimate, not a diagnosis, and is not a medical
device. Results are approximate and affected by lighting, movement,
finger placement and skin tone. Always confirm a concerning result with a
clinician and a real blood test.
