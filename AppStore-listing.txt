5. DESCRIPTION (4000)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Your heartbeat, in numbers — using nothing but your phone.

Some mornings you just want to know how you're doing before the day
runs away with you. ZeroSqueeze is a quiet, 25-second check-in for
exactly that. Lie back and rest the phone on your breastbone, and the
motion sensor reads the micro-vibrations your heartbeat sends into your
chest wall — seismocardiography, the same signal clinical SCG uses. Out
come your heart rate, HRV, breathing, ejection time, and a modelled
blood-pressure index — each one explained in plain words, all of it
worked out right on your phone. A fingertip scan adds hemoglobin.

No wearable. No chest strap. No cuff. No needle. No account. Nothing
ever leaves your device.

— A 25-SECOND CHECK-IN —
• Chest scan (SCG): lie back, rest the phone flat on your breastbone
  for about 25 seconds. ZeroSqueeze reads heart rate, beat-to-beat
  variability (HRV), breathing, left-ventricular ejection time and a
  blood-pressure index from your heartbeat's vibrations.
• Fingertip scan: cover the rear camera and flash for about 20
  seconds for a hemoglobin estimate with a confidence range, plus
  your pulse.

— YOUR DAY, AT A GLANCE —
Today greets you with a single Readiness score — one honest number
that pulls your recent readings together so you know whether to push
or to rest. No dashboards to decode, no fifty-stat wall. Just where
you stand this morning.

— NUMBERS THAT ACTUALLY MEAN SOMETHING —
Every reading comes with context: what's typical, where you land, and
what it hints at — the way a good coach would put it, not a spec
sheet. A live preview and a quality meter show you the moment the
signal is strong, so you're not guessing whether it worked.

— A MINUTE TO BREATHE —
When your numbers say slow down, ZeroSqueeze's guided breathing gives
you a calm pace to follow. Watch your HRV respond as you settle.

— NOTICE THE PATTERN —
One reading is noise; a trend is the story. Jot a quick daily
check-in and journal note, and Insights keeps a private history of
your heart rate, resting heart rate, HRV and hemoglobin so you can
watch the direction instead of chasing a single number.

— MAKE IT YOURS —
Got a recent lab CBC? Enter your real hemoglobin once and ZeroSqueeze
anchors future estimates to you, tightening the range over time.

— SHARE WHEN IT HELPS —
Heading to a check-up? Build a clean summary of your readings to
show a clinician or keep for yourself — on your terms, when you
choose.

— PRIVATE BY DESIGN —
ZeroSqueeze has no servers to call — there's no networking code in the
app at all. Every pixel is handled on your phone, no photo or video
is ever saved, there's no sign-in, and there's no tracking. Your
readings live on your device and nowhere else, locked when your
phone is.

— BUILT WITH CARE —
A warm, modern design with full Dynamic Type and VoiceOver support,
light and dark themes, and four simple tabs — Today, Scan, Insights,
Profile — so everything is one tap from where you are.

— PLEASE READ —
ZeroSqueeze gives a wellness estimate, not a diagnosis, and is not a
medical device. Results are approximate and affected by lighting,
movement, finger placement and skin tone. Always confirm a worrying
result with a clinician and a real blood test.

Review notes:
ZeroSqueeze is a wellness estimator. It uses the motion sensor
(seismocardiography, phone resting on the chest) to estimate heart
rate, HRV, respiration, ejection time and a blood-pressure index, and
the rear camera + flash (fingertip PPG) to estimate heart rate and
hemoglobin. There is no face/rPPG path. ALL processing is on-device:
there is no networking code, no account, and no data leaves the device.

ZeroSqueeze does NOT diagnose. Onboarding requires an "I understand
this is not a medical device" acknowledgement, and every result
screen repeats that it is a wellness estimate and to confirm
concerning results with a clinician.

To test (use a physical device — Simulator has no accelerometer or
camera): launch -> finish onboarding -> Today -> Scan tab -> "Chest
scan (SCG)" (rest phone on chest, ~25s) or "Fingertip Hb" (rear +
flash, ~20s). No login. Insights/trends are pre-populated with demo
data on first launch.
